Palmer Kane Design

Overview

Purpose

Palmer Kane Design is a small, local business offering many services including custom formal and bridal, alterations, mending, and childrens couture. This Website will serve as a hub for clients to gain valuable service information and get their project started with Palmer Kane Design.

Audience

Female, young in their 20s or 30s. Women planning events (weddings, dances, banquets). These women may know exactly what they want and they would like to get figures on expenses up front. Some may not know exactly what they need, so the service descriptions and quoting form will be important to them. Planning events or getting ready for them can be stressful so the audience needs to know that Palmer Kane Design will be there for them.

Typography

Heading Font: Dawning Of A New Day

This font captures the hand made feel of a seamstress shop and adds a little bit of class to the Titles and Headings.

Paragraph Font: Crimson Text

This font is very legible and this is needed for the bulk of the writing since the purpose of the writing is to be read.
