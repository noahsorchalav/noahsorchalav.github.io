Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar gaurantees the best experience on the best rapids in the North West. Our experienced guides will lead you safely through the wild rapids of the Salmon River on our fleet of newly updated rafts and kayaks. Bring the whole family as we have safety and fun for all ages and experience levels.

Audience

We invite anyone with a sense of adventure. Anyone who needs to escape! We are explorers, we are voyages, we are pioneers! Sons, daughters, mothers, fathers. We serve anyone with an upbeat pulse.
